About My SCHOOL

A Unique Heritage School .

Our school name is Government Utkrisht Senior Secondary School Chail Chowk . It is situated in chail chowk . There are around six hundred student in our school . Sh. Brij Lal is our school principal. He is very humble person and treat everyone with equality whether it is student or teacher. there are around 40 teachers in our school . Our school campus is devided into three parts. one of them is High school block, second is Arts and Commerce block and another is Science block. There are fifty rooms available in our school. There is also a library in our school where all the students can study and borrow books . Many extra curricular activities are organised in our school like Sports Tournament, Inter School Quiz Competition, NSS Camp , Inter house Competitions etc . Our teachers always give their endless efforts in teaching .Smart classrooms are also available in our school. Our school consist of many labs like I.T Lab ,I.T.E.S Lab, Security Lab, ICT Lab and science labs etc For us, our school is more than simply an educational institution it is also our second family which I established during our childhood. A family of wonderful friends, outstanding teachers, and fond school memories. We adore our school because it is where we learn how to be a good citizen and how to reach our goals. School is the only place where we can make friends without judging them.
